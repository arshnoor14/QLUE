# DataFluent Platform Rules and Business Guidelines

## 1. Commission Rates
- The DataFluent platform charges a flat 10% commission fee on all standard freelance transactions.
- Barter transactions (where students and providers swap services directly with no monetary exchange) are charged a 0% commission.
- Top Rated Providers receive a discounted platform commission fee of 7.5% (instead of 10%).

## 2. Refund and Cancellation Policy
- If a student cancels a gig transaction before delivery, they are eligible for a 100% refund.
- Refunds are credited to the student's digital platform wallet.
- Refund processing takes between 24 and 72 hours (1 to 3 business days).
- Completed transactions cannot be refunded directly through the automated system; they must go through the dispute resolution team.

## 3. Provider Tiers & Ratings
- Providers are rated by students on a scale of 1.0 to 5.0 stars after each transaction.
- "Top Rated Provider" status is automatically awarded to providers who maintain an average rating of 4.8 stars or higher, have completed at least 10 transactions, and have a completion rate above 90%.
- "Rising Star" status is awarded to new providers (joined in the last 90 days) who have completed at least 3 transactions with a 5.0-star rating.

## 4. Gig Rules & Pricing Limits
- Standard gig listings must be priced between a minimum of $5.00 and a maximum of $2,000.00.
- Any custom gig package priced above $2,000.00 is flagged as a high-tier transaction and requires manual approval by a platform administrator.
- Providers can create a maximum of 5 active gig listings simultaneously.

## 5. Dispute Resolution
- In the event of a service dispute, either the student or the provider can submit a dispute claim within 7 calendar days of the scheduled delivery date.
- Dispute claims must be backed by communication logs and draft files.
- The platform moderation team resolves all disputes within 5 business days, and their decision is final.
